# QuantCraft Curriculum Map

Phase 0: Mathematical maturity & numerical thinking  
Phase 1: Linear algebra & matrix methods  
Phase 2: Probability theory (measure-theoretic)  
Phase 3: Statistics, econometrics, time series  
Phase 4: Optimization, portfolio construction, risk  
Phase 5: Market microstructure & execution  
Phase 6: Machine learning for alpha (correctly)  
Phase 7: Stochastic calculus & derivatives  
Phase 8: Tail risk & robustness  
Phase 9: Systems engineering & autonomy

Progress is gated by understanding, not time.

Professional trading is:

- **Risk containment**
    
- **Behavior management**
    
- **Exposure control**
    
- **Constraint design**
    
- **Boredom**
    
- **Inactivity**
    
- **Survivability**

## The Role of the Trader (Redefined)

Anton makes the identity shift explicit:

> **The trader is not a decision-maker.  
> The trader is an operator inside a system.**

The system:

- Decides when trading is allowed
    
- Decides how much risk is permitted
    
- Decides when to stop
    
- Decides when to reduce exposure
    

The human executes — or does nothing.
